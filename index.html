Treatment & Procedures

Our experienced doctors and surgeons at Artemis Hospital, who specialize in different areas of heart care, diagnose and treat all kinds of heart diseases.

Coronary Artery Bypass Surgery

A surgical procedure to restore normal blood flow to an obstructed coronary artery.

Congenital Heart Surgery (ASD/VSD Surgery)

Surgical correction of heart defects that are present at birth affecting the structure of the heart.

Minimally Invasive Surgery

Advanced surgical techniques that require smaller incisions resulting in less pain and faster recovery.

Valve Repair

Procedures to repair or replace damaged heart valves to restore proper blood flow through the heart.

Pacemaker

Implantation of electronic devices that help regulate the heartbeat for patients with arrhythmias.

Heart Attack

Emergency treatment for acute myocardial infarction, including thrombolysis and primary angioplasty.
